🤖 How It Works

Upload a side-profile photo of your cow. The AI will:

① Segment the cattle silhouette using YOLOv8n-seg
② Extract body measurements from the mask (length, girth, proportions)
③ Apply the Schaeffer formula as a biomechanical prior
④ Refine the estimate with a trained Ridge regression model

Accuracy: R²=0.41, MAE ≈ 24.8 kg on the BMGF cattle dataset.

No depth sensor or scale required — just a standard photo.
